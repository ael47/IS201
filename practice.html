Here, I will highlight fun facts about sloths while practicing my IS 201 skills.

Sloths are fascinating creatures. While they are known for their slow lifestyle, there is more to them than meets the eye.

Here are some interesting facts about sloths:

  1. Where do they live?
    • Tropical rainforets
    • Latin and South America
    • Floppy ears
  2. What do they eat?
    • They are herbivores
    • They eat leaves from over 90 different trees
    • A favorite treat is hibicus flowers
    • It takes them 30 days to digest a leaf!
  3. Other random facts
    • They are blind in bright daylight
    • They can turn thier heads 270 degrees
    • Green algae grows on their fur and provides camouflage
